Usability

Usability refers to the extent to which a product or system can be used by users to achieve specific goals effectively, efficiently, and with satisfaction

It is a key aspect of user experience design and focuses on ensuring that the interaction between users and a product is seamless, intuitive, and user-friendly.

Dark Patterns

Dark patterns refer to deceptive and manipulative design practices employed in user interfaces, websites, and applications. These patterns are created to exploit users leading them to make choices that benefit the designer or the business behind the interface.

Dark patterns can manifest in various ways, such as misleading wording, confusing interfaces, hidden costs, forced subscriptions, or creating a sense of urgency.

Ethical Design

Ethical design is a holistic approach to creating websites, products, services, or systems that prioritize user well-being, respect user rights, and adhere to ethical principles.
